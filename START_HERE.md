## 🎯 The Construction CV Inspector Project

### **What It Is (In 30 Seconds)**
A FREE, open-source AI tool that automatically compares construction site photos with architect blueprints to find mistakes faster and prevent disputes objectively.

### **Who It's For**
Small contractors, site engineers, quantity surveyors in Nigeria (especially Lagos) who need quality inspections without expensive software.

### **What It Does**
1. ✅ Reads PDF blueprints (extracts all dimensions)
2. ✅ Analyzes site photos (with phone camera)
3. ✅ Uses AI to detect everything in photos
4. ✅ Automatically measures real vs planned
5. ✅ Shows: Matches ✅, Issues ⚠️, Problems 🚨
